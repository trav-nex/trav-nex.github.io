Industries

Wiil AI assistants are designed to handle the unique needs of different industries. Each solution combines conversational AI with industry-specific workflows and integrations.

Supported Industries​

IndustryKey Use CasesPlatform Services
HealthcareAppointments, prescriptions, insurance verificationAppointments
RestaurantsReservations, orders, menu inquiriesReservations, Menu Orders
RetailProduct discovery, orders, stock availabilityProducts, Inventory
Real EstateLead qualification, showings, property infoAppointments, Property Inquiry
AutomotiveTest drives, parts orders, service appointmentsAppointments, Products, Inventory
Beauty & CareService bookings, stylist selection, product salesAppointments, Products
Fitness & GymsClass bookings, training sessions, membershipsAppointments, Reservations
HospitalityRoom reservations, room service, spa bookingsReservations, Menu Orders, Appointments
Professional ServicesConsultations, client intake, callback schedulingAppointments

Channel Support​

All industry solutions support both communication channels:

ChannelDescription
Web ChatEmbed AI assistants on your website for text and voice conversations
Phone VoiceDeploy AI assistants to handle inbound phone calls

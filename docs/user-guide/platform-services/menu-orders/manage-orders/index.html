📋 Manage Orders

When your AI assistant takes a food order, it appears here immediately. The assistant already confirmed the order type (dine-in, takeout, delivery), asked for quantities, and captured any special instructions—you just need to prepare and fulfill the order.

Navigate to Bookings & Activity > Orders to access order management.

🔄 Workflow Overview​

StepActorAction
1️⃣👤 CustomerPlaces order via AI assistant
2️⃣🤖 AI AssistantConfirms items, quantities, order type
3️⃣🏢 BusinessReviews and confirms order
4️⃣👨‍🍳 KitchenPrepares the order
5️⃣📧 SystemNotifies customer when ready
info

Fast order confirmation is critical for food service. Customers expect quick acknowledgment, especially for delivery orders.

#MetricDescription
1⏳ PendingOrders awaiting confirmation
2👨‍🍳 In ProgressOrders being prepared
3✅ ReadyOrders ready for pickup/delivery
4🟢 CompletedSuccessfully fulfilled orders

🗂️ Order Tabs​

#TabDescription

3📋 All OrdersComplete order history including completed and cancelled

🍽️ Order Types​

Your AI assistant confirms the order type during the conversation:

#TypeDescription
1🍽️ Dine InCustomer eats at the restaurant
2☕ TakeoutCustomer picks up the order
3🚗 DeliveryOrder delivered to customer
tip

During AI conversations, the assistant asks customers about order type and quantity of items. This information is captured automatically in the order details.

➕ Create New Order​

Click + Add New Order for phone orders or walk-ins.

1️⃣ Customer Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Customer Name✅ YesFull name of the customer
2Phone Number❌ NoContact number for the order
3Address❌ NoDelivery address (if applicable)
4Order Type✅ YesDine In, Takeout, or Delivery
5Special Instructions❌ NoAny special requests or notes

2️⃣ Items Tab​

#FieldDescription
1Select ItemsChoose from your menu items
2QuantityNumber of each item

⚡ Order Workflow​

StepStatusAction
1️⃣PendingOrder received, awaiting confirmation
2️⃣ConfirmedOrder accepted by business

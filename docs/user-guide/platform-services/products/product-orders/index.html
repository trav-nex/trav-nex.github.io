🛒 Product Orders

When your AI assistant helps a customer purchase a product, the order appears here as "Pending." You review it, confirm payment, and update the status as you fulfill the order. Customers receive automatic notifications at each stage.

Navigate to Bookings & Activity > Orders to access product order management.

🔄 Workflow Overview​

StepActorAction
1️⃣👤 CustomerOrders via AI Assistant
2️⃣📥 SystemOrder appears as Pending
3️⃣🏢 BusinessConfirms and processes order
4️⃣📧 SystemCustomer notified
5️⃣🏢 BusinessFulfills and ships order
6️⃣✅ CompleteOrder marked complete

📊 Status Overview​

#StatusColorDescription
1⏳ PendingAmberAwaiting confirmation
2🔄 In ProgressBlueOrder being processed
3📦 ShippedVioletOrder shipped to customer
4✅ CompletedGreenOrder delivered and complete
5❌ CancelledRedOrder cancelled

🗂️ Order Tabs​

#TabDescription
1⏳ Pending OrdersOrders awaiting confirmation
2🔄 Current OrdersOrders in progress or shipped
3📋 All OrdersComplete order history

📋 Orders Table​

#ColumnDescription
1🔢 Order #Unique order identifier
2👤 CustomerCustomer name and email
3📦 ItemsNumber of items and product names
4💰 TotalOrder total with tax
5🔵 StatusCurrent order status
6💳 PaymentPayment status (Pending/Paid)
7📅 DateOrder date
8⚙️ ActionsView, edit, or update options

➕ Add New Order​

Click + Add Order to create an order manually. The modal has 3 tabs.

1️⃣ Customer Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Select Contact❌ NoChoose from existing contacts
2First Name✅ YesCustomer's first name
3Last Name✅ YesCustomer's last name
4Email✅ YesCustomer's email address
5Phone❌ NoCustomer's phone number

2️⃣ Products Tab​

#FeatureDescription
1🏷️ Category FilterFilter products by category
2📋 Product ListBrowse available products
3➕ Add ButtonAdd products to the order
4🔢 QuantityAdjust item quantities

3️⃣ Shipping Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Street Address✅ YesPrimary street address
2Apartment/Suite❌ NoAdditional address info
3City✅ YesCity name
4State✅ YesState or province
5ZIP Code✅ YesPostal code

📄 Order Details View​

Click any order row to view full details:

#SectionDescription
1📋 Order HeaderOrder number, status, and date
2👤 Customer InfoName, email, phone, customer ID
3📍 Shipping AddressDelivery address details
4📦 Order ItemsProducts, quantities, and prices
5💰 Order SummarySubtotal, tax, shipping, and total

⚡ Order Actions​

Update order status as you process and fulfill orders:

#ActionEffect
1✅ Confirm OrderMove from Pending to In Progress
2🔄 Mark In ProgressIndicate order is being processed
3📦 Mark ShippedRecord shipping with tracking info
4✅ Complete OrderMark order as delivered
5❌ Cancel OrderCancel with customer notification

💳 Payment Status​

#StatusDescription
1🟡 PendingPayment not yet received
2🟢 PaidPayment confirmed
3🔴 FailedPayment failed or declined
4🔵 RefundedPayment refunded to customer
info

When you update an order status, the system automatically sends a notification to the customer via their preferred communication channel (email or SMS).

tip

Orders placed by the AI Assistant start as Pending. Review the order details and click Confirm Order to begin processing.

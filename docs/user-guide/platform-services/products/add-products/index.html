📦 Add Products

Products are items your customers can purchase through your AI assistant. When a customer asks about product availability, pricing, or specifications, your assistant references this catalog to provide accurate, real-time answers.

Navigate to Service Management > Products to access the product catalog interface.

🖥️ Interface Overview​

The Product Management interface has two main tabs:

#TabDescription
1📦 ProductsView, add, and manage all products in your catalog
2🏷️ CategoriesOrganize products into categories for easy navigation

📋 Products Tab​

⚡ Action Buttons​

#ButtonDescription
1📥 Import ProductsBulk import products from CSV, Excel, or JSON files
2➕ Add ProductManually add a new product with full details

📊 Product Table​

#ColumnDescription
1🔵 StatusActive or Inactive product status
2📦 StockCurrent inventory quantity
3📝 Product NameProduct display name and SKU
4🏷️ CategoryAssigned product category
5💰 PriceProduct price
6📅 CreatedDate product was added
7⚙️ ActionsEdit, view, or delete options

🏷️ Categories Tab​

Organize your products into categories for better navigation and filtering.

⚡ Category Actions​

#ActionDescription
1👁️ View DetailsSee category info and products
2✏️ Edit CategoryUpdate name and settings
3🗑️ Delete CategoryRemove empty categories

➕ Create Category​

Click + Add Category to create a new category:

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Category Name✅ YesName for the category
2Description❌ NoCategory description
3Display Order❌ NoSort position in category list

➕ Add Product Form​

Click + Add Product to open the product creation form with four tabs.

1️⃣ Basic Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Product Name✅ YesDisplay name for the product
2Category✅ YesAssign to a category
3Description❌ NoProduct details and specifications
4Price✅ YesProduct price
5SKU❌ NoStock keeping unit identifier
6Brand❌ NoManufacturer or brand name
7Barcode❌ NoUPC/EAN barcode

2️⃣ Inventory Tab​

#SettingDescription
1Track InventoryEnable/disable stock tracking for this product
2Stock QuantityCurrent available stock (when tracking enabled)
3Low Stock AlertThreshold for low stock notifications

3️⃣ Physical Tab​

#FieldDescription
1WeightProduct weight
2UnitWeight unit (lbs, kg, oz, g)
3LengthProduct length dimension
4WidthProduct width dimension
5HeightProduct height dimension

4️⃣ Settings Tab​

#SettingDescription
1Active StatusProduct visibility in the catalog
2Display OrderSort position in product list

💡 Best Practices​

#PracticeDescription
1📝 Use Clear Product NamesNames customers would search for: "Wireless Earbuds Pro" not "SKU-WE-001"
2📦 Keep Stock UpdatedYour AI tells customers when items are out of stock—incorrect data frustrates buyers
3📄 Add Helpful DescriptionsInclude key specs so your AI can answer "Does this work with iPhone?"
4🏷️ Organize by CategoryHelps AI narrow down options when customers say "Show me your headphones"
tip

Products with Track Inventory enabled will show stock levels and alert you when running low.

info

Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*). Complete at least the Basic tab to save a product.

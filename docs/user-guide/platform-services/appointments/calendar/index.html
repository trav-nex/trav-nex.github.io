📅 Appointment Calendar

The calendar is your central hub for appointment management. When your AI assistant books an appointment during a customer conversation, it appears here instantly.

Navigate to Bookings & Activity > Appointments to access the appointment calendar.

📊 Dashboard Overview​

Monitor your appointments at a glance:

#MetricDescription
1📈 Total AppointmentsAll appointments in the selected period
2📆 TodayAppointments scheduled for today
3✅ ConfirmedAppointments confirmed and ready
4⏳ PendingAppointments awaiting confirmation

🗂️ Navigation Views​

Access appointments through three views:

#ViewDescription
1📅 Calendar ViewMonthly grid with status indicators
2⏰ UpcomingChronological list of future appointments
3📋 All AppointmentsComplete history with filtering and search

📅 Calendar View Features​

#FeatureDescription
1📆 Monthly GridAll dates visible with appointment indicators
2🔵 Today HighlightingCurrent date marked with highlight ring
3◀️ ▶️ NavigationArrow buttons to move between months
4👤 Customer NamesNames with color-coded status dots

Click any date to open a side panel showing all appointments for that day.

🎨 Status Colors​

#ColorStatusDescription
1🟡 AmberPendingAwaiting confirmation
2🟢 GreenConfirmedCustomer notified
3🔵 BlueCompletedService delivered
4🔴 RedCancelledAppointment cancelled
5⚪ GrayNo ShowCustomer didn't attend

➕ Add Appointment Manually​

Click + Add Appointment for walk-ins or phone bookings.

1️⃣ Customer Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Select Customer❌ NoChoose from existing contacts
2Customer Name✅ YesFull name of the customer
3Customer Email❌ NoFor appointment confirmations
4Phone Number❌ NoFor SMS notifications

2️⃣ Service & Details Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Service✅ YesSelect from configured services
2Date✅ YesAppointment date
3Time✅ YesAppointment start time
4Duration🔄 AutoFilled from service settings
5Total Price🔄 AutoFilled from service settings
6Deposit Paid❌ NoAmount already paid

🔗 Calendar Integration​

Sync your appointments with external calendars.

Supported Platforms​

#PlatformDescription
1📅 Google CalendarSync with Google Calendar and Gmail
2📧 Microsoft OutlookIntegrate with Outlook calendar

Integration Features​

#FeatureDescription
1🔄 Two-Way SyncAppointments sync in both directions
2⚡ Real-Time UpdatesChanges reflect immediately
3🚫 Availability BlockingExternal events block booking availability
4⚠️ Conflict PreventionAI checks availability before booking
tip

When connected, your AI assistant will automatically check your external calendar for conflicts before scheduling new appointments.

⚡ Quick Actions​

#ActionDescription
1🔗 Calendar IntegrationConnect external calendars
2➕ Add AppointmentCreate new appointment
info

All appointment data is stored securely and can be exported for reporting.

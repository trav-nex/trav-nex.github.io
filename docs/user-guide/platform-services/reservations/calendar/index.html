📅 Reservation Calendar

The built-in calendar provides a complete view of all reservations. Your AI assistant automatically books reservations here based on customer conversations.

Navigate to Bookings & Activity > Reservations to access the reservation calendar.

📊 Dashboard Overview​

Monitor your reservations at a glance:

#MetricDescription
1📈 Total ReservationsAll reservations in the selected period
2📆 TodayReservations scheduled for today
3✅ ConfirmedReservations confirmed and ready
4⏳ PendingReservations awaiting confirmation

🗂️ Navigation Views​

Access reservations through three views:

#ViewDescription
1📅 CalendarMonthly grid with capacity or individual views
2⏰ CurrentToday's and upcoming reservations sorted by date
3📋 All ReservationsComplete history with filtering, search, and export

📅 Calendar View Features​

#FeatureDescription
1📆 Monthly GridAll dates visible with reservation indicators
2🔵 Today HighlightingCurrent date marked with highlight ring
3◀️ ▶️ NavigationArrow buttons to move between months
4👤 Customer NamesNames with color-coded status dots

Click any date to open a side panel showing all reservations for that day.

🎨 Status Colors​

#ColorStatusDescription
1🟡 AmberPendingAwaiting confirmation
2🟢 GreenConfirmedCustomer notified
3🔵 BlueCompletedReservation fulfilled
4🔴 RedCancelledReservation cancelled
5⚪ GrayNo ShowCustomer didn't arrive

⚙️ Reservation Settings​

Click Settings to configure reservation behavior for each resource type.

🍽️ Tables Configuration​

#SettingDescription
1Capacity-BasedMultiple customers book same slot until capacity reached
2Resource-SpecificEach table booked by one customer at a time
3Default DurationSet default reservation length (hours)

🏨 Rooms Configuration​

#SettingDescription
1Capacity-BasedBook by total capacity (shared spaces)
2Resource-SpecificEach room booked exclusively
3Default DurationSet default stay length (nights)

⏱️ Rentals Configuration​

#SettingDescription
1Capacity-BasedMultiple bookings by capacity
2Resource-SpecificExclusive equipment bookings
3Default DurationSet default rental period

➕ Create New Reservation​

Click + Add Reservation to manually create reservations.

1️⃣ Customer Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Select Customer❌ NoChoose existing contact
2Customer Name✅ YesFull name of customer
3Customer Email❌ NoFor confirmations and reminders
4Phone Number❌ NoFor SMS notifications

2️⃣ Reservation & Details Tab​

#FieldRequiredDescription
1Reservation Type✅ YesTable, Room, or Rental
2Number of Guests❌ NoParty size
3Date✅ YesReservation date
4Time Slot✅ YesAvailable time slots shown with capacity
5Total Price🔄 AutoCalculated from resource settings
6Deposit Paid❌ NoAmount already paid
7Special Notes❌ NoRequests, dietary restrictions, etc.

⚡ Quick Actions​

#ActionDescription
1⚙️ SettingsConfigure reservation behavior
2➕ Add ReservationCreate new reservation
info

The day panel adapts based on your selected view mode, showing either capacity summaries or individual reservation details.

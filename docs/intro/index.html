Welcome to Wiil

Wiil is a Conversation as a Service (CaaS) platform that enables businesses to deploy AI-powered voice and chat assistants without writing a single line of code. Transform your customer support, sales, and operations with intelligent assistants that work 24/7.

🚀 No-Code

Deploy AI assistants without technical expertise

🕐 24/7

Always-on customer service across all channels

👥 Built-in CRM

Automated customer profiling and recognition

The Problem Wiil Solves​

Every business faces the same challenge: customers expect instant, 24/7 support, but staffing around the clock is expensive and impractical. Missed calls mean lost revenue. Delayed responses mean frustrated customers.

85% of customers whose calls go unanswered will not call back. Each missed call is a potential customer lost forever.

Wiil solves this by deploying intelligent AI assistants that:

  • Answer every call and chat instantly
  • Handle bookings, orders, and inquiries automatically
  • Qualify leads and collect customer information
  • Escalate complex issues to human agents when needed

Core Products​

Wiil offers two types of AI assistants that work together to cover all customer communication channels:

📞 Phone Assistant

Voice Calls
  • Dedicated phone numbers (local or toll-free)
  • Natural voice conversations
  • Multilingual support (20+ languages)
  • Smart call transfers to human agents
  • SMS follow-up capabilities
  • Call recording and transcription

💬 Web Assistant

Chat & Web Calling
  • Embeddable chat widget
  • Three modes: Chat, Web Call, or Unified
  • Customizable appearance and branding
  • One-click integration (WordPress, Shopify, React)
  • Real-time visitor engagement
  • Conversation history and analytics

Built-in CRM​

One of Wiil's most powerful features is its automated CRM. Unlike traditional systems where staff manually enter customer data, Wiil's AI assistant:

  • Auto-Qualifies Leads — Naturally requests name, phone, and email during conversations
  • Recognizes Customers — Identifies returning customers by name or phone number
  • Tracks Preferences — Records communication preferences for automated follow-ups
  • Sends Confirmations — Automatically emails or texts booking confirmations

Getting Started​

Ready to deploy your first AI assistant? Here's the path:

  1. Create your account — Sign up with email, Google, or GitHub
  2. Complete business setup — Select your industry and configure your business profile
  3. Configure your assistant — Choose voice, language, and upload your knowledge base
  4. Test and deploy — Preview your assistant, then go live with one click

What you'll need​

  • A Wiil account - Sign up for free
  • Basic understanding of your business requirements:
    • What tasks should your assistant handle?
    • Which channels will customers use to contact you?
    • What knowledge should your assistant have access to?
